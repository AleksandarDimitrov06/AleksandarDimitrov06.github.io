Sound files need to be placed in this directory with the following names:

1. move.mp3 - Sound for moving a piece
2. capture.mp3 - Sound for capturing a piece
3. check.mp3 - Sound when a king is in check
4. game-start.mp3 - Sound for starting a new game
5. game-end.mp3 - Sound for game ending (checkmate or stalemate)

You can get free sound effects from websites like:
- https://freesound.org/
- https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/
- https://www.zapsplat.com/

Download appropriate sound files, rename them according to the list above, and place them in this directory.
